Family

I love spending time with my family. I live with my wife — we don't have any children yet, but we're still a family. We really enjoy going on day trips to explore nearby places, having small private BBQs at home just for the two of us, and listening to music together.

Travel

I really love traveling to different cities and places. Exploring new places and understanding the local culture is something I truly enjoy, whether I'm alone or with others. One of the most iconic places I've been to is Chichén Itzá.

Motorbikes

I learned how to ride motorbikes when I was 11 years old. At the time, I wasn't even able to touch the ground while sitting on the motorbike. Nowadays, I enjoy exploring places where cars can't reach and feeling the air on my face while riding.

Hiking

I studied geology. While I was at university, I did a lot of field trips and used to do a lot of hiking and camping. That's where I got my love for hiking and exploring new places. What I enjoy the most about hiking is discovering places where only a few people have set foot.
